bash 內建支援 gettext，語法是

[[code]]
echo $"hello world"
[[/code]]

bash --dump-po-strings hello.sh 則會輸出

[[code]]

msgid "hello world"
msgstr ""
[[/code]]

script 的開頭要加上環境參數，就可以使用了～
export TEXTDOMAIN="hello"
export TEXTDOMAINDIR=`pwd`"/lang"
